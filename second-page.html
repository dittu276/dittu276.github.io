Humidity

As expected, the humidity increases significantly as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, homwever, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be less humid this time of the year than the northern hemisphere. This maybe due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered
